Cosmic Fields

A landmark which marks the location of the Transcendent Abyss/Ascendant Realm
The biome will be overridden by other biomes' terrain,
you can see an example in the image below.
Contains some chests, life crystals and hot springs.
These hot springs will regenerate your life faster and can be fished on to obtain cosmic fishes and gear.

Transcendent Abyss

An eerie, hostile and dark place filled with cosmic fauna.
Being in the abyss without an astronaut helmet will suffocate you, making you far weaker.
The abyss can be found above the Cosmic Fields.
Houses the cosmic archangel, who can be summoned at the abandoned Cosmic Cathedral.
Visiting the abyss at midnight might have some surprises...

Bright Galaxy Summon

A summon weapon dropped by the Celestial Seraph, the current final boss of my mod.

Summons a miniature galaxy to obliterate enemies
The galaxy becomes bigger and faster the more you summon
Right Click to turn the galaxy into a shield that can block up to 7 projectiles
The shield has a 15 second cooldown after breaking

The Volcanic Layer

A biome introduced by my mod that covers the region between Caverns and Hell.
Contains hellstone, carbon, ruby and new dangerous enemies.
The rocks will set you on fire, so be careful where you step, or use an obsidian skull.

Parrying

A mechanic added by my mod, that allows you to block attacks when timed perfectly.
You require a shield to parry, and you can obtain shields everywhere in the game from Pre-Boss to Post-Seraph.
(Omg that Sniper Rifle drop, how lucky!)

Laser Mining!

A Post-Moonlord pickaxe from my mod, that doesn't dig fast, but creates connecting laser nodes. These laser nodes clear any tile between them

Link


Tinkerer NPC

A Town-NPC that moves in after obtaining a modifier item.
She allows you to apply modifiers onto items and craft new items with upgrade items.
The prices are really high, but your items get a 75% discount for any future changes.
You can obtain her phone number later in the game, allowing you to use her services remotely. (With a higher price, of course.)

Atmospheron

Back to Transcendent Mod, the Atmospheron is a weather themed dragon with a fast-paced fight.
The boss has three phases:
- In the first phase it follows a set pattern and has 3 maxium stamina.

- In the second phase it has a random pattern and has 3/infinite (Expert and above)

- The final phase is kinda like Duke Fishron's, it teleports in a random direction and lunges at the player extremely fast 1/3/2 times.

I haven't really finished the boss yet, I'll update this section when it is.
