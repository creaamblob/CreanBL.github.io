Various Threats: Reunleashed


A Terraria 1.4.4 mod for tModloader that overhauls boss fights.

The development is currently paused (2024/12/16).

Reworks the following bosses:

- Eater of Worlds
- Wall of Flesh
- The Destroyer

Planned Reworks:

- Queen Slime
- Plantera
- Golem
- Lunatic Cultist

Adds a few items for the reworked bosses.
