Transcendent Mod


A Terraria 1.4.4 content mod for tModloader.

A mod that I started developing in 2022.

Contains:
- Plenty of Unique Weapons and Accessories
- 4 New Bosses and 3 Mini-Bosses
- Unique Mechanics
- Progression that goes beyond Moon Lord

- 2 New Vendors:
- The Blacksmith, capable of applying modifiers and upgrades to items
- The Transcendent Avatar, capable of doing stellar magic and selling cosmic items

- 2 New Biomes:
- Transcendent Abyss/Ascendant Realm
- The Volcanic Layer
