About Me

I'm CreanBL (Pronounced "Kriinbel"), a Finnish person with an interest in coding and gaming.

I currently code with C# and HTML, but I will seek education on game-development once I finish school. (2025, hopefully)

What inspired to start programming was watching some old Terraria modding tutorials as a child and going "Wow, I want to make that!"

Nowadays my skills have surpassed what I imagined back then by a lot.

I still have my first mod saved on my hard disk, and no, I will not share it because of embarrassment and legal concerns.
